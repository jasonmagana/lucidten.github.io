LUCID TEN's Founder - Carolina Huerta

Faced with adversity and tough obstacles in a community with limited resources, South Central Los Angeles, rose a young entrepreneur, Carolina Huerta. At the age of 22, Carolina commenced her journey to becoming a dedicated eyebrow artist.

Carolina’s path to becoming an eyebrow artist has not been a simple trek. While pursuing her dream to become one of the most prolific eyebrow artists in South Los Angeles, she has faced many obstacles along the way. Some of those hurdles include working other jobs to make ends meet. Although her journey to founding LUCID TEN has not been easy, her grit and determination has led her to the position which she is in now.

Carolina is an excellent example of showing that with hard work and determination anything is possible. Her role at LUCID TEN is providing the best service you can find in the region. Her attention to detail and her amazing skills are what attract clients. Carolina’s exceptional customer service skills and work ethic are what separates her from her competitors.
